BARBENHEIMER

A battle of mythological scale

The Battle

On July 21, 2023 two magnum opus films faced off at the box office. One a light-hearted satire and another a hard-hitting drama. One based on a toy line, one based on a historical figure. Behind the scenes, it was more than just two films facing off, but a director with his former ally of a production house: Christopher Nolan of Oppenheimer and Warner Bros. Pictures of Barbie.

Nolan and Warner Bros. Pictures parted ways after disagreements on the terms of release of Nolan's 'Tenet' (2021). Then on July 21, 2023, the two parties met once again at a clash at the box office.

The audience created the 'Barbenheimer' phenomenon due to the dueling contradictory aesthetics of the films. See how the films prepared for battle at the box office, on the biggest day for cinema in 2023.
